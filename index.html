News

DVRPC proudly serves as a resource for the region's media and keeps them apprised of our ongoing efforts to improve mobility, the environment and quality of life in the Delaware Valley. We regularly communicate with the area's approximately 300 media outlets, and encourage you to check out our up-to-the-minute news releases. For more information about DVRPC and its initiatives, contact Elise Turner, Communications Manager, at 215-238-2941.
